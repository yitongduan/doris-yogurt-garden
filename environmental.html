How to create a better yogurt?

GREEN'S Bioengineering CO., LTD registered in Canada in 1999, it is a modern biological dairy enterprise integrating r&d, production, sales and cold chain logistics. It is also the only large-scale dairy enterprise focusing on yogurt production in Canada. Over the years, the company has always adhered to the high grade, high quality, high standard product strategy, successfully created a series of yogurt products, and become the preferred brand of government reception, white-collar consumer market and high-end dairy consumption market.

Renewable Energy

In recent years, the warning signs brought by climate change to human beings have made governments all over the world think about how to reduce carbon and save energy. Doris's Yogurt Garden environmental protection team started wind power generation in 2014, and abandoning all non-renewable energy sources.

Environmentally friendly packaging

Doris's Yogurt Garden has been leading the world in the field of green packaging. All the outer packing all use environmental protection board, Most of this comes from natural plant fibers that are left over from organic skin care products. Packaging materials can be decomposed in nature and do not pollute the environment.
